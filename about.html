Our Story

Panda World Clothing was founded in 2020 with a passion for creating clothing style that combines Street wear and Hip Hop. Our journey began in Lebowakgomo and has since grown into a brand that values Quality and Uniquieness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide clothing that help our customers during the four seasons without compromise. We believe in equality and strive to make fashion accessible and inspiring.
